The executive dashboard provides a consolidated view of retail business performance. Sales trends reveal seasonal demand patterns, while regional analysis highlights strong and weak markets. Category profitability identifies profitable and loss-making products. Customer segmentation shows purchasing differences, and shipping analysis reveals operational bottlenecks. Discount analysis indicates that excessive discounts reduce profitability, while return analysis identifies areas requiring quality improvement. Leadership should focus on optimizing discounts, improving logistics, and investing in high-performing regions and customer segments. Future analysis should include forecasting and customer lifetime value.
